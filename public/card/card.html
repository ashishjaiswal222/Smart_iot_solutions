Arduino UNO

The Arduino UNO is a microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P. It is easy to use, perfect for beginners, and widely used in IoT and electronics projects.

Specifications:

  • Microcontroller: ATmega328P
  • Operating Voltage: 5V
  • Input Voltage: 7-12V
  • Digital I/O Pins: 14 (6 PWM outputs)
  • Analog Input Pins: 6

ESP32

The ESP32 is a low-cost, low-power system-on-chip microcontroller with integrated Wi-Fi and Bluetooth capabilities, making it ideal for IoT projects.

Specifications:

  • Processor: Dual-core Xtensa LX6
  • Clock Speed: Up to 240 MHz
  • Wireless: Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2
  • GPIO Pins: 34
  • ADC Channels: 18

Soil Moisture Sensors

Soil moisture sensors are used to measure the volumetric water content in the soil. They are essential for smart irrigation and agricultural applications.

Specifications:

  • Voltage: 3.3V to 5V
  • Current: Less than 20mA
  • Measurement Range: 0% to 100% soil moisture
  • Output: Analog and digital
